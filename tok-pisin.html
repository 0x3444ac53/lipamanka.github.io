What is Tok Pisin?

Tok Pisin is awesome, that's what it is.

What is the syntax of Tok Pisin?

SVO. pronouns (unless they're third person) mark the predicate themselves, and i marks the predicate in all other sentences. transitive verbs end with suffix -im (except for a few exceptions, including kaikai, the word for "eat") and are followed by the object. There are like three main prepositions that occur a lot by themselves, and the rest of the prepositions are almost always followed by long. etc.
